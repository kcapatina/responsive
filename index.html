Exclusive Merchandise

Introducing a new set of merchandise exclusive to the RIIZE PARK series, fans can now purchase photocard packs and collect unique keepsakes of their favorite member! Version A (We RIIZE ver.) consists of specialized photocards with the members dressed as their representative mascot. Verion B (WE LITTLE RIIZE ver.) has a more laidback look, with RIIZE dressed in varsity jackets and holding their mascot plush.

Each WE LITTLE RIIZE OFFICIAL TRADING CARD SET comes with 2 random photocards out of 15 along with a character lenticular or hologram card specific to the version. Watch as the idol transforms into their cute character, or get a rare unit card of the two of them!
